title: Data Rights & NFT Credits¶

Data Rights & NFT‑Backed Version Control¶

Sophía transforms knowledge into a digital asset using an NFT‑backed controlled version system:

  1. Contribution Capture — When you share expertise (labels, rationales, rules, policies), ecOS+LTM records the artifact and its provenance.
  2. Explainable Validation — Contributions are validated against explainable model behavior (SynA outputs, evidence trails, confidence).
  3. NFT Minting — A non‑fungible token is minted to represent the unique contribution version, with cryptographic links to inputs, outputs, and audits.
  4. Versioned Lineage — Subsequent edits mint new NFT versions, preserving complete lineage and authorship.
  5. Attribution & Credit — Each NFT embeds contributor addresses; downstream usage triggers automatic attribution and credit accounting.
  6. Privacy by Design — On‑chain records store only privacy‑preserving commitments; sensitive content remains encrypted or off‑chain.

Outcome: Every meaningful learning artifact becomes a traceable, ownable asset. Credit and compensation flow back to the humans who improved the model.
