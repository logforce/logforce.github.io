title: STRIDE Guardrails¶

STRIDE — Local‑First Zero‑Trust AI for AI¶

Goal: Make AI use on endpoints safe by default. STRIDE inspects inputs/outputs to/from local language/ML models and enforces policy before data reaches a model or leaves the endpoint.

Capabilities¶

  • Content & Safety Policies — Enforce organizational content rules at prompt and response time.
  • Data Loss Prevention — Detect/deny sensitive data movement.
  • Local‑first — Decisions happen on the endpoint, reducing latency and dependency on cloud.

Governance & Audit¶

  • Deterministic allow/deny with reasons.
  • Versioned policies and per‑decision evidence trails.
  • Continuous calibration with drift alerts.
